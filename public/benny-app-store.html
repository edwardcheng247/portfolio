      Get cash back on your groceries, no matter how you spend! Earn cash back when you shop brands on the app, earn 1-3% cash back on your EBT spending, and instantly check your SNAP & WIC balance. Benny is the all-in-one app to manage your groceries and EBT benefits to stretch your food budget. Save even more with the Benny Card: 1% back on groceries, gas, and public transit, and credit building, all for free with no credit check or other requirements. Join over 300,000 people who trust Benny to manage their benefits, save money, and get cash back. GET CASH BACK ON GROCERIES AND EBT, SNAP, & WIC Stop leaving money on the table. With Benny, you earn cash back every time you shop for groceries. Earn cash back from food brands like Kraft-Heinz, Nestle, Hot Pockets, Lunchables, Oscar Mayer, Kool-Aid, Olipop, and more, and get an additional 1-3% cash back on your SNAP benefits at major grocery stores and retailers, including: Walmart Kroger 7-Eleven Dollar General Albertsons Giant ...and hundreds more! Link your grocery account for automatic cash back and rewards, no pictures of receipts needed! EARN EVEN MORE WITH BENNY CARD Stop using debit and prepaid cards with no rewards. Loan money or direct deposit onto the Benny Card and get 1% back on groceries, gas, and public transit. Build your credit for free, no credit check or other requirements. CHECK YOUR EBT BALANCE INSTANTLY Never guess your balance at the checkout line again. Benny is the fastest and easiest way to check your EBT balance and transaction history 24/7, right from your phone. Check Your Balance: See your SNAP balance and WIC balance in seconds. View History: Track all your EBT card food and cash transactions. Save Time: No more calling numbers or saving paper receipts. Always know your total benefits. FIND REWARDS, DEALS & EBT PROVIDERS Maximize your benefits! Benny helps you find exclusive cash back deals on individual food items like drinks, snacks, and more. New rewards are added weekly. SAFE, SECURE & TRUSTED Your money and personal information are protected by best-in-class encryption and security features. Benny uses bank-level encryption to keep your EBT card data safe and private. NATIONWIDE COVERAGE Benny supports EBT card programs (including SNAP, WIC, and Summer EBT) in all 50 states, DC, Puerto Rico, the US Virgin Islands, and Guam. Check your EBT balance for all major providers, including: ebtEDGE ConnectEBT California CalFresh Texas Lone Star Card (Your Texas Benefits) Florida & Pennsylvania ACCESS Cards Illinois Link Card Massachusetts DTA Connect Oregon Trail Card
